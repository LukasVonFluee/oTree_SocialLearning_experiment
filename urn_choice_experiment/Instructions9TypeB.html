Setup for Decision Task

  • During the experiment, participants will choose between the two urns multiple times after they have been shuffled.
  • Every time a participant chooses an urn, a marble is randomly drawn, and each time a marble is drawn, it is returned to the urn.
  • Depending on the colour of the drawn marble, participants can earn points.
  • We will explain more about who can earn points for which colour later.
