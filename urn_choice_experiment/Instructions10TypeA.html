Instructions - Decision Task

  • After the urns have been shuffled, each participant has to choose between the two urns.
  • When a participant chooses once between the urns, 1 marble of the chosen urn will be randomly drawn, and each time a marble is drawn, it is returned to the urn before the next marble is drawn.
  • The colour of the marble that has been drawn from your chosen urn is revealed before the marble is put back into the urn.
  • Depending on the colour of the drawn marble, participants can earn points.
  • After the colour of the drawn marble has been revealed, the colour is hidden again, and the marble is put back into the urn.
